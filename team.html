Our Team

Meet the creators behind Paiwt - The Art Creation Game.

Daniel Crosswaters Bohnett

The creator of ArtWaters and Paiwt, Daniel is an American designer who works in art and entertainment. For years Daniel researched and developed the correct formulations for a safe, mess free project he named ArtWaters. He created a non-toxic, water soluble product that is a great way to ease feelings of stress as an outlet for self-expression. Through his hard work and dedication, ArtWaters has unlimited artwork project potential beyond beautiful paintings, for example, jewelry, ornaments, and table tops. Simply put, ArtWaters has everything an artist needs to start creating one-of-a-kind original abstract artworks.

Learn more about Daniel.

Mark Stephen Bohnett

Mark is an active member and a contributor in the art-community. His early life was spent at the Toledo Museum of Art School from 1957-1967. He is former Juried Member of City Art San Francisco. Mark’s experience allows him to build on the best principles as well as deliver with the latest innovations.
